About Traffic Road

Traffic Road is a 3D, fast-paced, first-person motorcycle driving game where your goal is to navigate through heavy highway traffic while avoiding collisions and scoring points for high-speed, skillful riding. Do not forget to brake when needed!

4 Game Modes & A Flurry of Driving Challenges

Multiple game modes, each with unique missions, bring added challenges and extra thrills to the gameplay experience. That’s true! In Traffic Road, reaching the finish line on time is just the beginning; there are tougher challenges (or easier ones) waiting in its multiple modes.

  • Career mode takes you to race through a series of levels. Missions are completed by reaching the finish line before time runs out or by fulfilling other specific objectives revealed at the start of each level.
  • Endless mode pushes your focus and reflexes to the limit: Navigate as far as you can without crashing, and see how long you can survive!
  • Time Trial mode tests your speed and precision under time pressure and try to cover the best distance possible.
  • Free mode lets you unwind and explore with no pressure, just pure discovery. It allows you to roam the world at your own pace without limits. Simply go as far as you want.

How to Play Traffic Road

Drive your motorbike through busy urban roads, highways, or deserts and avoid crashes with other vehicles. By performing rewarding reflexes, precision, top speeds, and skillful dodges along the way, you will earn a lot of points, in-game money, and bonuses.

Still, this game is not just about driving. It also lets you build an impressive collection of unlockable and upgradable motorbikes. With 30 captivating models to choose from, you can fill your garage with dream vehicles by completing engaging missions and earning as much in-game money as possible.

Spend your earned money wisely, either on upgrading the vehicle’s power, handling, and braking or on buying more powerful models. Don’t just focus on driving and racing; aim to boost its performance.

Game Controls:

  • Up arrow key: Accelerate
  • Down arrow key: Brake
  • Right and left arrows: Steer right or left.
  • Q or E: Look to the left/right.

Ready to jump in, race your motorbike through busy streets, dodge unpredictable traffic, and tackle challenging routes that demand not just quick reflexes but also speed and precision? Play Traffic Road and conquer its various game modes.
